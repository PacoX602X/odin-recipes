Slow Cooked Beans

Ingridienrs:

  • Any amount of beans.
  • 2 tspn of salt and paper per pound of beans
  • Aromatics

Instructions:

  1. Soak beans overnight.
  2. Strain and rinse beans and place in slow cooker.
  3. Add aromatics.
  4. Add enough water to cover beans.
  5. Cook on low for 6 hours.
  6. Serve with warm tortillas and enjoy!

Homemade Corn Tortillas

Ingridients:

  • 2 cups of masa
  • 1 1/2 to 2 cups of hot water
  • 1 teaspoon of fine sea salt

Instructions

  1. Mix a cup of water into the masa. Add water as needed.
  2. Rest the dough for 10 minutes.
  3. Portion the dough into 40 gram balls.
  4. Press balls with a tortilla press.
  5. Cook tortilla in a non-stick pan for about 40-60 seconds per side.
  6. Serve with our slow cooked beans and enjoy!
